Crystal Lake

Crystal Lake is a private lake located south of Maltby adjacent to the Snohomish-King County line. The lake is 54 acres and has a maximum depth of 32.8 feet. There are extensive wetlands north of the lake. A private, gated residential community surrounds the lake on the remaining three sides.
